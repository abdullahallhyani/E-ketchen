our business

Headquartered in Saudi Arabia, Kitchen Retail is one of the major retail chains in the Middle East and the largest retail chain in the Kingdom with around 200 stores in 44 cities. Kitchenette operates two types of stores; Hyper kitchen (supermarkets) supermarket kitchen. With more than 120 million customers visiting our stores every year, we operate 4 major distribution centers in the Kingdom with a fleet of more than 650 trucks to deliver 70,000 varieties of food and non-food products to our stores. Since 2009 we have also offered more than 400 of our own brand products to serve our customers as a trusted retailer in a number of food and non-food categories. More than 20,000 employees work with dedication and commitment in order to meet the shopping needs of our customers.
